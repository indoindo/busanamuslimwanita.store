The ingredients needed to cook Bacon Carbonara:
  1. You need 200 grams Pasta
  2. Get 250 ml All Purpose Cream
  3. Use Parmesan Cheese
  4. You need Bacon sliced into bits
  5. Prepare Butter
  6. Get Onion
  7. You need McCormick Carbonara mix
  8. Get slices Mushroom
Steps to make Bacon Carbonara:
  1. Melt butter in a heated pan
  2. Cook the bacon bits, onion and mushroom slices in the melted butter then set aside
  3. Pour the Cream in a saucepan. Add the McCormick carbonara mix and stir until dissolved in a medium heat
  4. Sprinkle parmesan cheese onto the the mixture and continue to stir until it boils. Add parmesan cheese depending on the taste desired
  5. Pour the carbonara sauce on the cooked pasta
