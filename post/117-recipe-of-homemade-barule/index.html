The ingredients needed to cook Barule:
  1. Provide baby potatoes
  2. Provide gram flour
  3. You need orange food colour
  4. Prepare red chilli powder
  5. Provide chat masala
  6. Get salt
  7. Provide oil for frying
Instructions to make Barule:
  1. First boil the baby potatoes.
  2. Add boiled potatoes,gram flour,red chilli powder,salt and some water in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add food colour and mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a wok,add this potatoes deep fry till golden brown in colour.
  5. Serving in a bowl,spread green chutney and sprinkle chat masala.
