The ingredients needed to prepare Chicken Tikka Masala:
  1. Provide Chicken thigh, bone in and skin on
  2. You need English spinach leaves
  3. Take Tomatoes, chopped
  4. Use Pure cream
  5. Get Spring onion
  6. Prepare brown onion, chopped
  7. You need (CURRY PASTE)
  8. Get garlic, peeled
  9. Use Thumb size knob ginger, peeled
  10. Get Cayenne pepper
  11. Use Smoked paprika
  12. Provide Garam masala
  13. Take Sea salt flakes
  14. Prepare Peanut oil
  15. Prepare Drizzle of sesame oil
  16. You need Tomato paste
  17. Provide Long red chilli
  18. Take Fresh coriander
  19. Use Dedicated coconut
  20. Get Ground almond
  21. Get Cumin seeds
  22. Take Coriander seeds
Steps to make Chicken Tikka Masala:
  1. Firstly- make your curry paste. In a fry pan throw all you dry herbs and spices and toast until aroma is released and smells delicious.
  2. Now tip them into food processor with the rest of the the curry paste ingredients. Blitz until you have created a smooth paste.
  3. Rub into chicken and allow to marinate for as long as you can (I left mine over night).
  4. Now, pre-heat oven to 180*C.
  5. Throw chicken into a casserole dish along with tomato & onion and cook for1 hour.
  6. Now to stir in your cream (stir well) and cook for a further 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Towards the end of cooking the chicken, get your rice done and ready to go.
  8. Remove chicken from oven, fold in the spinach and spring onion. Plate the chicken topped with creamy sauce and brown rice on the side. Scrumdiddly!!! So good!!
