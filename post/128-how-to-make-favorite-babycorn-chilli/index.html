The ingredients needed to prepare Babycorn chilli:
  1. Get sweet baby corn
  2. Provide white flour
  3. Take corn flour
  4. You need black pepper powder
  5. You need red chilli sauce
  6. Provide white vinegar
  7. Get finely chopped garlic
  8. Prepare grated ginger
  9. Prepare tomatoes ketchup
  10. You need salt
  11. Take Cooking oil for deep frying
  12. Get finely chopped green chilli
  13. Take light soya sauce
  14. You need orange food colour
Steps to make Babycorn chilli:
  1. Firstly heat the steemar then put on babycorn and steem it 5 min on medium flame
  2. In a mixing bowl add white flour, corn flour, black pepper powder to taste salt mix it well now add some little water mix it properly make a thik consistency batter without any lumps
  3. Now take a baby corn then drop in batter and properly coat it
  4. Now drop in hot oil and deep fry until it crispy and golden brown in colour now remove the plates and keep it a side
  5. In a bowl add tomato ketchup, red chilli sauce, vinegar, light soya sauce, pinch orange food colour, 4tbsp corn flour,some little water mix properly
  6. Now heat the wok with oil then add garlic saute until it becomes golden nice brown colour then add ginger green chilli stir it few minutes on lower flame
  7. Now add prepared mixture and fried babycorn some little water mix it properly stir it 2or 4 min on lower flame now gravy until it becomes thick then reduce the flame
  8. Now It is ready to serve
