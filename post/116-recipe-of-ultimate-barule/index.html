We hope you got benefit from reading it, now let’s go back to barule recipe. To cook barule you only need 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you do it.

The ingredients needed to make Barule:
  1. Take baby potatoes
  2. Prepare besan
  3. Take maida
  4. Get red food colour
  5. Provide red chilli powder
  6. Provide chat masala
  7. Provide Salt
  8. Get Oil for fry
Steps to make Barule:
  1. Boiled the potatoes
  2. Add besan, maida, red food colour, Red chilli,salt in boiled potatoes mix well
  3. Heat oil in Wok add potatoes and deep fry till crispy
  4. Now add green chutney and chat Masala serve hot
