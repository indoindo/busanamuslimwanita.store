The ingredients needed to cook Parmesan Crumb Roasted Potatos:
  1. Provide baby yukon gold potatos, pre cooked for 7 minutes in boiling salted water and drained, then cut in half.
  2. You need bakery croissant pulsed in food processor to make course crumbs
  3. Get minced cloves of garlic
  4. Use fresh grated lemon zest
  5. Provide cajun seasoning
  6. Provide black pepper and salt to taste if needed
  7. Get olive oil
  8. You need butter, melted
  9. Get chopped frrsh parsley
  10. Take grated parmesan cheese
  11. Prepare sliced gree onions
Steps to make Parmesan Crumb Roasted Potatos:
  1. Preheatvven to 450
  2. Combine in a large bowl the croissant crumbs, garlic, lemon zest, pepper, cajun seasoning and oil and butter mix to combine. Add potatos and toss to coat. Place potatos in a single layer, cut side down on baking sheet.Sprinkle tops with the crumbs left in the bowl.
  3. Roast 10 minutes, add parmesan cheese and parsley and roast 5 tob10 minutes more or until golden and crispy.Remove to a platter garnish with green onions and serve hot!
