The ingredients needed to prepare Easy Marble Cake #HealthyBakingContest:
  1. Prepare self-raising flour
  2. Use caster sugar
  3. Prepare cocoa powder
  4. Take butter/margarine
  5. You need eggs
  6. You need vanilla extract/essence
  7. Get milk
  8. Use For the icing (adjust accordingly)
  9. You need icing sugar
  10. You need butter /margarine
  11. Prepare Green food color
  12. Use Vanilla essence/lemon juice
  13. Take tsps Water/milk-optional
  14. Use Sprinkles for decoration
Steps to make Easy Marble Cake #HealthyBakingContest:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees or 350 degrees F and grease your pan.
  2. Sift your flour and castor castor sugar. Mix with a whisk.
  3. Melt your butter or margarine. It should not be too hot
  4. Beat your eggs and vanilla essence in a different bowl till frothy
  5. Add your melted butter to the eggs.
  6. Create a hole in the middle of the flour mixture.
  7. Pour in your wet ingredients and gently fold the mixture, a little at a time. Use a spatula for best results
  8. Add the milk to the mixture. Be careful not to over-fold the mixture.
  9. Divide your batter into two. In one mixture add the cocoa powder.
  10. Spoon half of the vanilla batter into the baking tin, followed by 2/3 of the cocoa powder mixture. Add the rest of the vanilla batter and finish with the cocoa batter mixture.
  11. Place it in the second oven rack at the middle for 45-55 minutes.
  12. For the icing, mix the icing sugar, green food color, and butter till stiff. Use a spatula or knife to apply on top of the cake. Add some sprinkles.
  13. Enjoy!
