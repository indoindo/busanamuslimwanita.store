The ingredients needed to make Chicken vindaloo:
  1. Provide Boneless, Skinless Chicken Brest
  2. Get Can Of Tomato Sauce
  3. Provide Vindaloo Seasoning. Most Spice Stores Will Have This. If Not Mix Coriander,garlic,cumin,ginger,cinnamon,mustard,cayenne,jalapeño, Cardamom,turmeric, Black Pepper And Cloves.
  4. Take Cubed Potatoes
  5. Prepare Onion
  6. Get Red Wine Vinegar
  7. Provide Salt
  8. Provide Water
Instructions to make Chicken vindaloo:
  1. Brown the cubed potatoes and set aside.
  2. Brown the chicken, add it to a soup pot
  3. Mince and brown the onion
  4. Mix tomato sauce and vindaloo seasoning into a paste. You can add more or less of the seasoning depending on how spicy you want it.
  5. Add the paste to the onion and mix it in
  6. Add the water, salt,and vinegar. Stir to combine, pour over the meat.
  7. Cover and cook over low heat for 30 min
  8. Add potatoes cook uncovered over a medium heat for 20-30 minutes, or until the potatoes are done
