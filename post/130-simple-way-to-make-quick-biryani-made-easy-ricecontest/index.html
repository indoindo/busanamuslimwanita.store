The ingredients needed to make Biryani made easy #ricecontest:
  1. Prepare rice
  2. Prepare grade chicken marinated
  3. Provide ground cumin, paprika, tandoori masala
  4. Use cinnamon(optional)
  5. Get large potatoes and 1 pinch of salt and sunset yellow food color
  6. Provide large onions
  7. You need natural yoghurt or Mala
  8. You need large tomatoes
  9. You need salt
  10. You need Cooking oil for deep frying the potatoes&frying chicken & onion
  11. Use small piece of raw pawpaw
  12. Prepare cinnamon sticks or a safron stick- optional
Steps to make Biryani made easy #ricecontest:
  1. Shallow fry the marinated chicken. I marinated mine using lime juice,coriander and 1 teaspoon of mixed spice..until tender but not too soft.
  2. Shallow fry your onions.
  3. I slightly boiled my potatoes and added a pinch of salt then coated with the food colour and deep fried them.
  4. Blended the tomatoes, yoghurt and diced pawpaw.
  5. Stir fry the spices then add the onions and the yoghurt mixture.once hot. add in your chicken and bring it to boil
  6. When almost boiled add in your fried potatoes for 2-3min.
  7. The rice cook it separately, I boiled 4 cups of water then added a pinch of salt and my washed rice. So we used 1 1/2cups normally white rice and added 1/2cup of multi grain japanese rice for a twist but that's optional and the cinnamon sticks/safron stick.
  8. Serve…bon appetite
